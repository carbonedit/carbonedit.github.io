Editing Services

Carbon Edit provides editing and proofreading of chemistry manuscripts. Our editors are native English speakers who are experienced with the conventions and style of chemistry articles published in prestigious journals. We will work with you to ensure that your writing clearly communicates your accomplishments to the broader scientific community.

Types of documents edited:

  • Research articles
  • Review articles
  • Grant proposals
  • Theses
  • Fellowship applications

Editing services include:

  • Correcting spelling and punctuation errors
  • Checking for grammatical errors, such as incorrect verb tense or conjugation
  • Improving word choice and sentence construction
  • Eliminating unnatural or awkward phrasing

Please note that our editing services do not include translation of the text from a foreign language, alterations to the formatting of the document, or editing and verification of the references.

Privacy

We are committed to the privacy of our clients, and all submitted manuscripts remain strictly confidential. Client names and email addresses are not shared with third parties.
