Request a Quote

The first step in the editing process is obtaining a quote for our editing services. The free quote we send you will include an order form listing the cost and the number of business days required for editing. If the terms are satisfactory and you would like to enlist our editing services, you can return the signed order form to us by email.

To obtain a free quote, please email us at editor@carbonedit.com with the document as an attached Microsoft Word file. We will respond with a quote for our services within two business days. Presentations can be emailed as Powerpoint or Keynote documents.
