Frequently Asked Questions

If your questions are not answered below, please contact us.

Why should I choose Carbon Edit?

Carbon Edit employs chemists with advanced degrees from the highest-ranked institutions in the United States. Our editors are experienced in working with both native and non-native English speakers to ensure that their research accomplishments are communicated clearly to the scientific community. We provide high-quality services at competitive rates and with fast turnaround times, and we will work closely with you to improve the quality of your writing. All services, manuscripts, and client information are kept strictly confidential.

What types of documents do you edit?

Carbon Edit will edit many kinds of chemistry documents, including research articles, review articles, grant proposals, theses, dissertations, fellowship applications, poster presentations, and slide presentations. We can edit documents in a variety of subfields in chemistry, including but not limited to: organic chemistry, inorganic chemistry, physical chemistry, analytical chemistry, materials chemistry, and biochemistry. If you are unsure if our editing services would be appropriate for your manuscript, please contact us.

How does the editing process work?

Our editing process proceeds as follows:

1) The manuscript is submitted to Carbon Edit for review.

2) Carbon Edit proofreads and edits the manuscript, and returns the manuscript with comments and/or clarifying questions for the authors' consideration.

3) The author accepts or rejects the suggested changes and provides information necessary to clarify the manuscript.

4) Carbon Edit performs a second round of editing, and returns a final version of the manuscript.

How much does editing cost?

Rates depend on the length of the document and the extent of editing required. We are happy to provide you with a free quote describing the costs and turnaround time for editing your document.

How long does editing take?

For typical orders (less than 10,000 words), editing will require 3-5 business days. Rush orders (2 business days) may be available for an additional fee. Quotes for our services will include the time required to edit your document.

How should documents be submitted for editing?

Documents should be emailed to us as Microsoft Word documents. Any accompanying figures should be included to help our editors better understand your work. Our editors use the "track changes" feature to provide in-text comments and corrections to your document. Presentations can be sent as Powerpoint or Keynote documents.
